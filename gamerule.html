Game objective


Be the first player to complete one or more brand identities by drawing cards from each category (Logo, Colors, Typography, Slogans) through challenges like 'Draw me a logo,' Quizzes, and 'Guess the right logo.'

Game Elements



Goose brand game board

Online Timer and Dice

Challenge cards

Brand identity cards

Set-up



Each player chooses a pawn

Shuffle Challenge cards face-down on the corresponding board square

Place Brand Identity cards on the board

Place dice within reach

Game Turn


  • Roll dice to advance
  • Move pawn clockwise
  • Depending on the square, draw the corresponding challenge cards
  • If successful, draw a brand identity card
  • Player to the right takes the next turn

Box/Card Types



End of Game



First to complete a brand identity (or several, depending on the choice of brands per person), wins."
